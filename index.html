Platform

From Field Data to Maximised RDTI Refunds.

SENTRL transforms site activity into structured R&D evidence. Technicians capture GPS-stamped photos, variations, failure modes and labour logs at the source, generating audit-ready narrative for advisors.

Powered by the SENTRL framework (Systems Engineering Network for Testing, Research & Laboratories), the platform enforces disciplined engineering documentation without slowing down your crews.

01

Audit-Ready Documentation

Structured capture of installation variations, experiments, design changes and outcomes. Every entry links to a project, asset and hypothesis, forming a defensible R&D record aligned with ATO expectations.

02

Immutable Field Evidence

GPS coordinates, timestamps, photo streams and labour hours are logged in one pass, on-site. Evidence is traceable to individual staff and locations, providing an immutable trail for audits.

03

Structured RDTI Evidence Capture

Activities, costs and time entries align to RDTI claim categories. SENTRL exports into audit-ready packs for your R&D consultants and accountants, closing the gap between field teams and finance.
